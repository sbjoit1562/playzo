About PlayZO

Our Story

PlayZO was founded with a simple yet powerful idea: to make opinion trading accessible, fun, and rewarding for everyone. We saw a world full of passionate individuals with strong insights on everything from sports to entertainment, and we wanted to create a platform where those opinions could be put to the test. Our journey began in a small garage, fueled by coffee and a shared vision to revolutionize how people engage with current events.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals to capitalize on their knowledge and intuition. We believe that well-informed opinions have value, and we've built a secure, skill-based environment where you can trade on future events. We are committed to fairness, transparency, and creating a vibrant community of thinkers, predictors, and winners.

Rahul Tripathi, the visionary behind PlayZO, is a passionate entrepreneur with a deep-seated belief in the power of knowledge.
